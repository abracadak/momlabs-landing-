§ 04

Equity Swap Option (JV)

Alternative to upfront-royalty model: Joint Venture with equity participation.

4.1 JV Structure

REGIONAL PARTNER

  • Establishes local entity in target market
  • Provides capital, operations, local sales network
  • Holds 70–80% equity
  • Manages P&L and local compliance

MOMLABS

  • IP contribution as equity investment
  • Holds 20–30% equity (IP in-kind)
  • Technical advisory & quality control
  • Dividend share from JV profits

4.2 When to Choose JV vs License

CriterionLicenseEquity Swap (JV)
